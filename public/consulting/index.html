Consulting


I co-founded DobleA LAB , an applied research workshop that bridges academia and industry. DobleA LAB helps organizations translate information needs into actionable research outcomes, integrating rigorous methodological foundations with practical industry experience.

Services

Applied Research Workshop — an intensive program covering the complete research cycle, delivered in four evening sessions (hybrid format):

  1. From client inquiry to study design
  2. Instrument design and measurement quality
  3. Data production and analysis
  4. Results interpretation and decision-making

On-demand specialized workshops for organizations, covering survey design, data quality, statistical analysis, causal inference, experimental design, A/B testing, sample weighting, and research software (R, Python).
